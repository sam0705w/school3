  • Attendence

    Availing extra holidays is against the School's philosophy and the principles of teaching and learning. ■ The minimum expected attendance for all students is 87%. In case, the attendance falls below this, parents would be required to meet the Principal. ■ In the event of any sudden requirements, such as emergencies in the family etc. parent would be required to justify their child's absence with an application and a meeting with the Principal. ■ Those children who remain absent for even a single day, must get a note from the parents, citing the reason for their absence, duly signed by the parents. ■ In case of protracted sickness, which ought not to happen, an application must be sent to the Principal, explaining the reasons of such absence along with a Medical Certificate from a registered medical practitioner. Such requests shall be dealt on the merits of the case. ■ Absence on account of celebrations, marriages or any other functions within the family is required to be discussed with the Class Teacher prior to the leave.
